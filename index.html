Types of Provision

Study Abroad

“Study abroad” is a chance to pursue your college studies in a foreign country.

Dual Degree

A Dual Degree is where you study two different fields, one after the other, and receive two separate degrees.

Joint Degree

A joint degree is one where you study two different subjects at the same time. These are then combined into a single qualification.

Student Exchange

student exchange is a program in which students from an educational institution travel to a foreign country as a student of their institution's partner institution in the host country.
